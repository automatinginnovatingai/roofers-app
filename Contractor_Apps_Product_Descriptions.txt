Automating Innovating AI – CONTRACTOR APPS
==========================================

Production Manager App  
The Production Manager App is built for fiberglass insulation contractors, gutter installers, and multi‑trade crews who need a fast, accurate, and organized workflow system. Track jobs, materials, labor hours, payroll, and production output in one place. Ideal for companies handling insulation, gutters, or both.

Electrician App  
The Electrician App is designed for residential and commercial electrical contractors who need clean job tracking, material management, labor logging, and payroll accuracy. Perfect for electricians managing rough‑ins, trim‑outs, service calls, and new construction projects.

Gutter App  
The Gutter App is engineered specifically for gutter installation companies. Track linear footage, coil usage, materials, labor hours, job details, and payroll with precision. A streamlined tool for gutter pros who want speed, accuracy, and a paperless workflow.

Plumbers App  
The Plumbers App is built for plumbing contractors of all sizes. Manage rough‑ins, service work, materials, labor, job costing, and payroll in one unified system. Ideal for residential, commercial, and new‑construction plumbing operations.

Handyman App  
The Handyman App is made for handyman businesses, home repair specialists, and multi‑skill service pros. Track jobs, materials, labor hours, client details, and invoices with ease. Perfect for general repair, carpentry, drywall, flooring, and multi‑trade service work.

Roofers App  
The Roofers App is built specifically for roofing contractors who need a fast, accurate, and field‑ready workflow system. Track tear‑offs, installations, repairs, roofing materials, labor hours, jobsite details, and payroll in one unified platform. Ideal for shingle, metal, tile, and flat‑roof operations. Supports roofing‑specific units such as squares, bundles, rolls, and linear footage, with automated pay calculations and clean Excel exports.

Payroll App  
The Payroll App is built for contractors who need fast, accurate, and automated payroll processing. Calculate hourly pay, piece‑rate, job‑pay, overtime, double‑time, blended‑rate premiums, and custom overtime rules with ease. Use it as a standalone payroll engine or connect it to any AIAI CRM App—including Gutter, Plumbing, Roofing, Handyman, Electrician, and Production Manager—for seamless payroll integration across all service lines.

Each app is built for a specific trade, but all share the same clean, efficient workflow system. Contractors who operate in multiple service lines can combine apps for a unified business management experience.
